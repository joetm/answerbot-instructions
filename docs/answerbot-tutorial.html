AnswerBot Tutorial

Before we start, we need to familiarize you with a search tool.

Your task in this tutorial is to find an fast way to lose weight that has a high potential for long-term success.

AnswerBot is a crowd-supported decision support tool.
Please open the link to AnswerBot now.

On AnswerBot, move the sliders to select the criteria.
Then read and explore the results.

Hints:
Leaving the slider in the middle position means 'neutral'.
The 'distance' parameter tells you how good the result matches your selected criteria. The lower the value, the better.

If you are not sure how to use AnswerBot or have further questions, please ask the instructor.

Once you are familiar with AnswerBot, confirm the checkboxes below and press the 'Start Task' button to start your tasks.
